ATEEZ's Achievements!

  • Rookie of the Year: ATEEZ received several "Rookie of the Year" awards in 2018 and 2019 from various South Korean music award shows, recognizing their impact in the industry shortly after their debut.
  • Asia Artist Awards (AAA): ATEEZ received the "Rising Star Award" at the 2019 AAA, further cementing their status as emerging talents in the K-pop scene.
  • Seoul Music Awards: ATEEZ has been nominated for several categories at the Seoul Music Awards, including the "New Artist Award" and "Bonsang (Main Prize)".
  • Gaon Chart Music Awards: The group has been recognized at the Gaon Chart Music Awards with nominations for categories like "New Artist of the Year.".
  • Mnet Asian Music Awards (MAMA): ATEEZ has received several nominations at MAMA, one of the most prestigious K-pop award ceremonies, including "Best New Male Artist.".
  • Soribada Best K-Music Awards: The group has been nominated for categories such as "New Hallyu Rookie Award" at the Soribada Awards.
  • Billboard World Albums Chart: ATEEZ has achieved success internationally, with multiple albums charting on the Billboard World Albums Chart.
  • Fandom Growth Award: ATEEZ's fanbase, known as ATINY, has grown rapidly and received recognition for their passion and dedication to supporting the group.
