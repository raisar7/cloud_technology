Hongjoong!

Hongjoong, whose real name is Kim Hongjoong, is the leader and rapper of the South Korean boy group ATEEZ. Born on November 7, 1998, he is known for his charismatic stage presence and unique fashion sense. Hongjoong is also a talented composer and producer, contributing to ATEEZ's music through his songwriting skills. He has played a pivotal role in shaping the group's identity with his strong leadership and creativity, making him a respected figure in the K-pop industry.

Yunho's passion for his craft, commitment to ATEEZ's success, and dedication to perfection in dance have garnered him a dedicated fan following and respect in the K-pop industry.
